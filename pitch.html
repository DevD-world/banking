Completion IQ

Pitch Walkthrough

Demo

1. Problem

Applications die because documents are missing, follow-ups are delayed, and ownership becomes unclear.

2. Solution

Completion IQ sits between customer, RM, and CRM to continuously answer what is missing, who must act, and which applications are at risk.

3. AI Middleware

OCR + document intelligence classifies forms, extracts fields, scores quality, detects signatures, flags mismatches, and raises notifications.

4. RM Workflow

Scan documents, send WhatsApp reminders, create customer upload links, sync CRM, run KYC, and move complete applications to approval.

5. Manager Control Tower

Revenue leakage, branch heatmap, SLA tracker, RM productivity, review queue, and root-cause intelligence.

6. Business Outcome

Higher application completion rates, reduced revenue leakage, better customer acquisition ROI, and faster loan/deposit conversion.
